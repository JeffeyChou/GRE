<pre><strong>devolve</strong></pre>
[dɪ'vɒlv]
(1)v. to pass on from one person or entity to another （权利，责任等）下放(2)v. to gradually go from an advanced state to a less advanced state [[衰]]落
推荐：de-向下 + volv-转，表示向下流转，转交。
